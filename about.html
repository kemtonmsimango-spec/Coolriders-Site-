About Coolriders

Your Local Transport Partner

Dependable rides for locals and visitors in the Western Cape.

Coolriders Hermanus provides dependable charter services and professional local transport for guests, visitors, and residents throughout the Western Cape. Operating with a commitment to reliability and comfort, we ensure every journey is seamless and stress-free.

As a trusted partner for premium guest houses, hospitality venues, and private clients, we specialize in a range of services from quick local rides to dedicated long-distance transfers. Whether you need transport for a private event, an airport run, or dependable daily travel, our fleet is ready to serve.

Our experienced drivers also provide dedicated support for curated wine tours and scenic local experiences, allowing you to explore the region safely. At Coolriders, our focus is simple: delivering a comfortable, punctual, and highly professional transport experience tailored to your needs.
